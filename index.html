About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

Wiilly-i-am

Hi, I'm Wiilly-i-am. I break things, I build things, I automate what I can, and I like to get a understanding of how things actually work behind the scenes.

You can normally find me tinkering with cybersecurity tools, hacking hardware, reverse engineering something arbitrary, or scripting something to make life easier. I like to break things, poke networks, and push technology to its breaking point—whether it's code, electronics, or infrastructure.

I have a special interest in offensive security—red teaming, exploit dev, adversarial research, and all the other things that go into stress-testing systems and keeping defenders on their toes.

My repositories are a combination of open-source utilities, experiments, and things I figured would be handy (or simply entertaining). I work in Python, Bash, PowerShell, C++, and JavaScript a lot, but really, I'll use whatever gets the job done.

If you're interested in hacking, automation, hardware, networks, offensive security, or just neat tech in general, you'll likely find something here you enjoy. Feel free to star, fork, or get in touch if you'd like to work together or just chat about geeky stuff.
